Plastic pollution in the ocean.

According to World Wildlife Forum, there's been an estimate of around a 100.000 marine mammals being killed every year, due to ingestion or entanglement in plastic artifacts.

Thus not all species are being affected, WWF states that all seven sea turtle species are affected by the pollution.

To sealife, it can be hard to distinguish a plastic bag from a jellyfish or other prey which can cost it it's life.
